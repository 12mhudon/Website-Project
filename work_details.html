Civic Nation

Work Scope

Video // photo // graphic design // web maintenance

Work Details

Civic Nation houses initiatives that address public issues by keeping them at the forefront of public discourse. As a content strategist, my main goal was to assist different teams in engaging their online audiences with various streams of content - from full project rollouts for timely engagements to evergreen videos and graphics that further strengthened their online brands.

Initiatives included Joe Biden's It's On Us, ALL IN Campus Democracy Challenge, the United State of Women, and #VoteTogether.
